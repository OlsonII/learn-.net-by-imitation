********************* Veterinary Manager ********************************

This project wil be a veterinary manager with the next features

Clients
Register Clients to know who are the pets owners

Doctors
Register doctors to know who are attending the medical appointments

Pet
Register pets to give to it the medical services, every pet belong to their owner

Services
The veterinary offer two services, medical appointments and aesthetic services. These services can has the next status
Programed, OnDate, Lose, Canceled and Completed and every service has a price that is calculated based on the pet size.
